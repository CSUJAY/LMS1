Incident Overview

This section introduces the basics of incidents, including types and causes, and the importance of quick response to mitigate damage.

Incident Response Process

Effective incident response involves detection, containment, and analysis of the threat to minimize damage and restore operations.

Recovery Strategies

Recovery strategies include restoring data from backups, repairing affected systems, and re-securing the network after an incident.

Post-Incident Actions

Post-incident actions, such as documentation, reporting, and review, help improve future responses and reduce the likelihood of recurrence.
