Course Overview

This course introduces key concepts of cybersecurity principles, focusing on security fundamentals and risk management.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of cybersecurity.
  • Identify common cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities.
  • Learn about risk management and mitigation strategies.
  • Familiarize with security frameworks and best practices.
