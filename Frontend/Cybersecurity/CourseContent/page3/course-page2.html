Overview of Threats and Vulnerabilities

This section will cover various threats faced by information systems and their vulnerabilities.

Types of Threats

  • Malware: Software designed to harm, exploit, or otherwise compromise systems.
  • Phishing: Deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information by masquerading as a trustworthy entity.
  • DDoS Attacks: Overloading a service or network to make it unavailable to users.

Understanding Vulnerabilities

Vulnerabilities are weaknesses in a system that can be exploited by threats. It's essential to identify and mitigate them.

Impact of Threats

Threats can lead to data breaches, financial loss, and damage to an organization's reputation. Understanding their impact is crucial for mitigation.

Preventive Measures

Implementing security policies, training employees, and using security tools can help mitigate threats and vulnerabilities.
